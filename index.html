Notes

  • Durable white clothes - hot wash, cold rinse
  • Durable colours - warm wash, cold/warm rinse
  • Anything absorbant OR moisture wicking (towels, gym clothes, period pants ets), do NOT use softener as it will interfere with fabric technologies
  • Gentle detergent is best EXCEPT when dealing with heavy soiling on durable material
Key
  • Delicate wash: Short, cold wash, slow tumble/spin that mimics hand-washing
  • Heavy duty: For sturdy, heavily soiled items. Use a long cycle, high speed tumle/spin and warm/hot wash
  • Rinse and spin: Quickly rinses and removes moisture from swimwear/towels, no detergent
  • Pre-wash: Stained/heavily soiled, pre-wash to soak stains before cycle
  • Extra rinse: Additional rinse at end of cycle to ensure dirt, dust and detergent are flushed out
  • Speed/quick wash: For lightly soiled items/small loads
